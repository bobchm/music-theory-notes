Degrees of the Scale

The numbers and names of the notes of the scale are referred to in multiple places and are worth knowing.

  1. First - Tonic, or key note
  2. Second - Supertonic
  3. Third - Mediant
  4. Fourth - Subdominant
  5. Fifth - Dominant
  6. Sixth - Submediant
  7. Seventh - Leading note
